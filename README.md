## 🎮 Gameplay

## Pick Your Mode First
When you open the game, you'll see 3 buttons. Tap the one that matches your environment:

- **🤫 Silent Mode** — you're in a quiet room. Just whisper or make small sounds to jump
- **🗣️ Normal Mode** — normal room with some background noise. Speak clearly to jump
- **💀 Rage Mode** — noisy/crowded place. You need to actually shout to make the character jump

---

## Make a Sound to Start
After picking your mode, the game waits. Make any sound and your character starts running automatically.

---

## How to Control

| What you do | What happens |
|-------------|-------------|
| Stay silent | Character walks slowly |
| Talk normally | Runs faster |
| Make a loud sharp sound | Character JUMPS |
| YELL | Character flies upward |
| FULL SEND SCREAMING | Rage mode — screen goes red, max speed |

The louder and sharper your sound, the higher the jump. A quick "HA!" works better than a long drawn-out sound.

---

## Avoid the Cactuses
Orange cactuses come from the right. Jump over them by making a sharp loud sound at the right moment. Don't jump too early or too late — timing is everything fr.

Every 100 points the speed increases. It gets genuinely unhinged after score 200 💀

---

## Scoring
- Pass a cactus without hitting = +points + anime wow sound 🎵
- Hit a cactus = game over + cat laugh sound 😭
- Tap anywhere on game over screen to restart and pick mode again

---

## Tips
- Short sharp sounds work best for jumping — think clap, snap, or "HEY!"
- In Silent Mode even a whisper triggers a jump so stay quiet between jumps
- In Rage Mode you genuinely need to scream — your neighbors will know 💀
- Watch the **VOICE METER** bar at the bottom to see how loud you're being in real time
